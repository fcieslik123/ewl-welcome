On Arrival

Preliminary Information

Upon arriving at Warsaw Chopin Airport, you must:

  1. Pass through passport control
  2. Collect your luggage
  3. Proceed to the designated meeting point
⚠ Please note: If you arrive in Poland via a country that is part of the EU (European Union), you will not need to go through immigration control again — with the exception of Turkey, the United Kingdom (England), and others. Please check in advance.

Mon–Fri Arrivals

Your First Steps in Poland

01

Contact the Arrivals Coordinator

When you reach the meeting point, contact Filip immediately. WhatsApp: +48 695 230 867

02

Exchange Currency

We recommend exchanging your currency to Polish Złoty (PLN). Note that airport exchange rates may be less favourable — do not exchange large amounts at the airport.

03

Buy a Local SIM Card

Purchase a local SIM card for internet access: Orange, Play, Plus, T-Mobile — available at STARTER or RELAY stores in the airport. You must show your ID to register. If you don't buy one at the airport, a SIM card will be provided at the Warsaw arrivals office.

04

Follow the Exit Signs

Follow the signs towards the city exit (wyjście/exit).

05

EWL Coordinator Will Help With Your Taxi

The EWL coordinator will help you order a taxi to the hostel (if you need to stay overnight) or to the EWL office (if the contract will be signed the same day).

Sat, Sun & Holidays

Weekend & Holiday Arrivals

If you arrive on a weekend (Saturday–Sunday) or a public holiday, you will need to arrange your own taxi and book accommodation independently.

How to Get to the EWL Office

Step 1

📍 Location

Enter Eurocentrum, Tower Alfa. Find the building at Al. Jerozolimskie 136 and enter through the door marked "alfa".

Step 2

🛂 Reception

Show your passport and say "E-VU-EL". The receptionist will open the turnstile towards the lifts.

Step 3

🛗 Lift

Take the lift to Floor 15. Press the up arrow button in the corridor, then press 15 inside the cabin.

Step 4

🚪 Entrance

Find the door with the two-hands logo and press the white button on the left to ring the bell.
